What is Eyebrow Microblading?

Eyebrow microblading is a semi-permanent cosmetic tattooing technique that creates the illusion of fuller, more defined eyebrows. Using a specialized hand-held tool with ultra-fine needles, our certified artists meticulously deposit pigment into the superficial layers of your skin, mimicking individual hair strokes. The result is incredibly natural-looking brows that frame your face beautifully.

Benefits of Microblading:

  • Achieve perfectly shaped and symmetrical brows.
  • Natural-looking, hair-like strokes.
  • Long-lasting results (typically 12-18 months).
  • Wake up with flawless brows, saving time daily.
  • Waterproof and smudge-proof.

The Lexuscare Microblading Process

1. Consultation & Design

We begin with a thorough consultation to discuss your desired shape, color, and preferences. Our artist will map and draw your ideal brow shape for your approval.

2. Pigment Application

Once satisfied with the design, we apply a topical numbing cream to ensure comfort. Our certified artist then meticulously creates hair-like strokes using sterile, disposable tools.

3. Aftercare & Follow-up

We provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing and pigment retention. A complimentary touch-up session is scheduled 4-6 weeks later for perfect results.

At Lexuscare, your safety and satisfaction are paramount. We use only premium, hypoallergenic pigments and strictly adhere to the highest hygiene and sterilization protocols. Our artists are internationally certified, ensuring you receive expert care and natural-looking results every time.

Microblading FAQs

Is Microblading painful?

Most clients experience minimal discomfort, often describing it as a scratching sensation. We use a high-quality topical numbing cream to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure, making it a generally tolerable experience.

How long do Microblading results last?

Microblading results typically last between 12 to 18 months, depending on factors such as your skin type, lifestyle, sun exposure, and how well you follow aftercare instructions. A touch-up session is usually recommended annually to maintain the vibrant look.

What is the healing process like?

The initial healing process takes about 7-14 days, during which your brows may appear darker and then gradually lighten. Full pigment stabilization can take up to 4 weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure optimal healing and color retention.

Who is a good candidate for Microblading?

Microblading is ideal for individuals looking to enhance their brows, fill in sparse areas, or redefine their shape. It's suitable for most skin types, but a consultation is essential to assess your eligibility, especially if you have sensitive skin, certain medical conditions, or are on specific medications.
