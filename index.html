Frequently Asked Questions

Is Microblading painful?

Most clients experience minimal discomfort, often describing it as a scratching sensation. We use a topical numbing cream to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure.

How long do results last?

Microblading typically lasts 12-18 months, while lip tinting can last 1-3 years. Longevity varies based on skin type, lifestyle, and aftercare. A touch-up is usually recommended after 4-6 weeks for optimal results.

What is the aftercare for a Hydra Facial?

After a Hydra Facial, avoid direct sun exposure, harsh exfoliants, and excessive sweating for 24-48 hours. Keep your skin hydrated and follow our specialist's personalized product recommendations for best results.
