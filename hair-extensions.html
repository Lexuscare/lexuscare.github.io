What are Hair Extensions?

Hair extensions are a popular and versatile way to instantly add length, volume, and even color to your natural hair. At Lexuscare, we offer a range of high-quality, ethically sourced hair extensions, including clip-ins, tape-ins, micro-links, and fusion methods. Our expert stylists will consult with you to determine the best type of extension and application method to suit your hair type, lifestyle, and desired look, ensuring a seamless and natural blend.

Benefits of Hair Extensions:

  • Instant length and volume.
  • Experiment with new styles and colors.
  • Cover thinning areas or add fullness.
  • Boost confidence with a luxurious mane.
  • Versatile styling options.
  • High-quality, natural-looking results.

Hair Extensions FAQs

How long do hair extensions last?

The lifespan of hair extensions varies depending on the type of extension, hair quality, and how well they are maintained. Typically, permanent extensions (tape-ins, micro-links, fusion) can last from 6 weeks to 6 months with proper care and maintenance appointments.

Will extensions damage my natural hair?

When applied correctly by a certified professional and maintained properly, hair extensions should not damage your natural hair. We use safe application techniques and provide thorough aftercare instructions to ensure your hair's health and integrity.
